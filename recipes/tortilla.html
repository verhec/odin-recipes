Spanish Tortilla

Designed by Freepik

Description

This is a simple traditional dish if you like spanish cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 750g Potatoes
  • 8 Eggs
  • 1 Onion
  • Olive Oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Pepper
  • 80g Manchego-Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees (top and bottom heat).
  2. Peel off the skin of the potatoes and onions and cut them into thin slices.
  3. Put some olive oil into an oven-safe pan and steam the onions for 3 minutes until they are glisten - after that put them into a bowl.
  4. Now, put the potatoes into the pan with some olive oil and fry them 10 minutes until they are soft.
  5. Sprinkle the salt and pepper on the potatoes, mix it and put them into a second bowl.
  6. Stir the eggs in a separate bowel, add the potatoes and onions and mix it together.
  7. Put half of the mass into the hot pan. Keep in mind that everything in the pan needs to be flat.
  8. Spread the cheese over the potatoes and onions in the pan.
  9. Let it fry for 10 minutes nut do not turn it.
  10. Put the pan into the preheated oven and bake it for 30 minutes. There should be a nice golden crust.
  11. Let it cool down for 5 minutes and cut it into 6 - 12 pieces. Enjoy the meal!
