Spaghetti with Shrimps and Tomatoes

Designed by Freepik

Description

Cooked pasta panned in olive oil with shrimps and fresh tomatoes. Fresh parsley compliments this flavorful dish.

If you like seafood and pasta, this recipe is for you!

  • 500g Spaghetti
  • Olive oil
  • 30 Shrimps (frozen)
  • 6 Garlic cloves
  • 30 Cocktail tomatoes
  • 2 Chilies
  • 6 Spring onions
  • Parmesan
  • Parsley
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and Pepper
  1. Let the shrimps thaw.
  2. Chop the garlic and the chilies.
  3. Cut the spring onions into small rings.
  4. Halve the cocktail tomatoes.
  5. Set up pan and let the olive oil heat up. Once it is ready put the vegetables into the pan.
  6. Now, add the thawed shrimps to the vegetables in the pan, put the lid on it and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Cook the spaghetti according to the description on the package.
  8. Chop the parsley.
  9. Add salt, pepper and lemon juice to the sauce in the pan.
  10. When the spaghetti are ready, drain them and put them into the pan and mix it.
  11. Lastly, serve the dish on a plate and sprinkle some parmesan and parsley on it - ENjoy the meal!
