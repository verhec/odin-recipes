Guacamole served with Nachos

A nice snack for a party or watching a movie.

  • 2 Avocadoes
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • Lemon Juice (1/2 Lemon)
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 1 tablespoon Yoghurt
  • Salt and Pepper
  1. Mash the avocadoes with a fork.
  2. Cut the tomatoes in very small pieces.
  3. Chop the garlic cloves.
  4. Add the tomatoes, the lemon juice, the garlic and the yoghurt to the avocadoes.
  5. Mix it and season it with salt and pepper.
  6. Enjoy the dip with your nachos.
